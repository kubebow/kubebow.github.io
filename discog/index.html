Θ SP = Single | LP = Album | EP = Like an album, but small
Θ BX = Bonus Tracks | Album durations exclude bonus tracks
Θ S/T = Self-Titled | T/T = Title Track | B/W = Backed With
Θ Snippet = Small demo/teaser track included as a bonus track | Album durations count these
Θ If SP comes with multiple versions, duration only refers to the original.
Θ MV = Music Video | Non-MV videos may contain some basic moving visuals
Θ Dates are MM/DD/YYYY because I'm a smelly American.
Θ YouTube Video = Manual video upload, Full LP | YouTube Music = Tunecore upload, Playlist for LP

Θ All projects have free mp3 downloads. Free projects are available in wav and flac formats as well.
Θ I'm trying to release one project per month. If a project is released later than the end of a month it will have an asterisk (*) in the release period.
